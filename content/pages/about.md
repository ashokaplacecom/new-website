## Our Mission

The Placement Committee is at the heart of Ashoka's commitment to student success beyond the classroom. We strive to create a seamless interface between the academic excellence of our students and the diverse requirements of the professional world. Something to test ts out.

## The Team

Our committee consists of dedicated students from diverse academic backgrounds, working together across outreach, resources, and logistics modules.

### Outreach

Building and maintaining relationships with top-tier organizations across consulting, finance, social sector, and technology.

### Resources

Providing students with the tools they need — from resume workshops to industry-specific mentorship.

### Logistics

Ensuring recruitment drives, both on-campus and virtual, run smoothly and efficiently.
