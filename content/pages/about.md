## Our Mission

The Placement Committee of Ashoka University is a student-run body dedicated to facilitating career opportunities for the graduating class. We work closely with the Office of Career Services to ensure every student has access to the resources, mentorship, and opportunities they need.

## How We Work

We operate as a bridge between **recruiters** and **students**. Our team manages outreach to companies, organizes pre-placement talks, coordinates interview schedules, and provides end-to-end support throughout the recruitment cycle.

### For Students

- Personalized career guidance and mentorship
- Resume and cover letter workshops
- Mock interview sessions with industry professionals
- Access to our curated job and internship board

### For Recruiters

- A diverse pool of talent from India's leading liberal arts university
- Seamless online and on-campus recruitment processes
- Dedicated points of contact for every recruiting partner
- Flexible engagement models — placements, internships, and live projects

## Get In Touch

Interested in recruiting from Ashoka or want to learn more about what we do? Reach out to us at **connect.placecom@ashoka.edu.in** or visit us at the Office of Career Services.
