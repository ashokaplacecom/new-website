The Placement Committee is a student-run body at Ashoka University — a diverse group of 20+ individuals united by one goal: ensuring every Ashoka student has the resources, connections, and opportunity to thrive in their career.

We work year-round across **Outreach**, **Resources**, **Logistics**, and **Tech & Operations**, collaborating closely with students, faculty, and recruiting partners to make placements seamless and impactful.
